Grant Ready Impact

Our Mission

Lee-Bella's No Place Like Home CDC is being built to provide safe, nurturing, dependable 24-hour childcare for working families and essential workers.

The Problem We Solve

Many families struggle to find reliable childcare during overnight or nontraditional work hours. This creates barriers to employment, stability, and peace of mind.

How Funding Helps

Grant funding will support licensing, safety systems, classroom furniture, learning materials, and startup costs needed to create a warm, secure, development-focused environment for children.

Community Impact

Our center will support workforce stability, essential workers, and families who need childcare options that match real-life schedules.
