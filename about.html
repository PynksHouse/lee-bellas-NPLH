About the Center

Gwinnett County's answer to the childcare gap

Lee-Bella's No Place Like Home CDC will be a licensed Child Development Center operating 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, 365 days a year in Gwinnett County, Georgia. We are built specifically for the families who have been left behind by traditional daycare hours.

Our Mission

"To provide safe, nurturing, and exceptional childcare to families in Gwinnett County around the clock — so that no parent ever has to choose between their livelihood and their child's wellbeing. At Lee-Bella's, there truly is no place like home."

Gwinnett County is one of the fastest-growing counties in the United States, with over 950,000 residents — tens of thousands of whom work non-traditional hours. Night-shift nurses, first responders, hotel workers, warehouse staff, and single parents currently have virtually no reliable childcare after 6:00 PM. Lee-Bella's fills that gap with warmth, professionalism, and a genuine commitment to family.

The center will begin by leasing a 1,500–2,500 sq ft commercial space, serving 15–25 children with plans to expand. Every aspect — from our flexible enrollment options to our door-to-door transportation service — is designed with the working family in mind.
