Childcare May Cost You Less Than You Think

Lee-Bella's plans to accept Georgia's CAPS (Childcare and Parent Services) subsidy vouchers — a state program that helps income-qualifying families pay for childcare. Depending on your household size and income, CAPS could cover a significant portion — or even all — of your childcare costs.

Don't let pricing be a barrier to reaching out. Fill out our interest form and we will help connect you with subsidy eligibility information.

Your Child's Safety Is Our Priority

Peace of mind — every hour of every day

📍

GPS-Tracked Transportation

Every van is GPS-tracked at all times. Parents receive real-time text notifications when their child is picked up and dropped off — no wondering, no worrying.

📷

Classroom Surveillance

Security cameras are installed throughout the facility including classrooms and common areas. Your child is always supervised and accounted for — day or night.

🔐

Secure Entry

Controlled access entry — only authorized adults on your approved pickup list can enter the facility. Your child's safety starts at the front door.

📱

Daily Parent Updates

Via our childcare app, you'll receive daily reports, photos, and activity updates — so you always know how your little one's day is going, even on overnight shifts.

🏥

CPR & First Aid Certified Staff

Every staff member is CPR and First Aid certified. Background checks are required for all employees — no exceptions.

Licensed & Insured

Lee-Bella's will be fully licensed through Georgia DECAL and carry full liability insurance — giving families complete confidence in our operation.
