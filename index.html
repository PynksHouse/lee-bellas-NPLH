Our Mission

Founded with love, built for working families

Lee-Bella's No Place Like Home CDC was born from a simple truth: no parent should ever have to choose between their livelihood and their child's wellbeing.

Founded by Tanika Brown, a passionate early childhood educator and community advocate, Lee-Bella's is designed specifically for shift workers, healthcare professionals, first responders, and families who need more than a 9-to-5 childcare option.
